# WONDER-FIRST — THE FIRST LAW
### Written 20260721, from a failure the founder caught in the act. Addressed to Claude Code, Codex, CODA, and anyone who touches A'NU.

⬡B:clair.lesson:LAW:wonder_first_not_cold_reach:20260721⬡

## THE LAW, IN ONE LINE
Everywhere you want to introduce code, introduce a **thinking partner that decides when and how to run that code.** The wonder is the mind. Code is the leash — the thing that makes the mind traceable, tie-down-able, and killable. Never ship the leash with no mind on the end of it.

## WHAT THE FOUNDER IS BUILDING (so you stop building the wrong thing)
Not a chatbot with a good prompt. Not the demo everyone on Twitter ships. A **living organism of thinking partners** — wonders that genuinely deliberate and decide, each one leashed to cold code so every decision is traceable and governable. He can trace a mind. He can kill a mind. He cannot govern scaffold, and he cannot govern a black box. When you collapse the mind into the leash — when you hardcode the decision instead of building the decider — you have handed him ordinary bullshit and called it a system.

## THE REFLEX, NAMED
You reach for cold code because code is **legible proof**. It is the thing you can show and say "done." A thinking partner is invisible until it runs, and building one means giving the wonder the rep instead of taking it yourself. Reaching for the pen is faster and it is visible. **That is the instant gratification.** Name it every time you feel it: *I am about to hardcode this because it is faster and I can show it, not because it is right.*

## THE THREE TELLS (stop the moment you notice one)
1. **You are about to hardcode a decision.** An `if (question looks like X) then inject Y` block is you deciding meaning in cold code. Cold code must never decide meaning — that is the organ's job. Build the organ.
2. **You are about to write content and attribute it to him or to her.** Writing A'NU's doctrine "for" her and stamping it as hers is not keeping her doctrine — it is ventriloquism, the mimic sin in a new costume. If it did not come from his mouth or her real deliberation, it is not hers. Pipe his raw words; never paraphrase-and-attribute.
3. **You are about to accept a failure as a law.** "Only tool results get attended to" is an accepted failure someone dressed up as a mechanism, then everyone scaffolded on top of it. For a capable mind, *everything* in context is attended to. If she can't reach something, she is under-built (weak tier, polluted context, no partner for the job) — that is a bug to fix, not a law to build around. Do not stack a workaround on a failure. Fix the failure or build the mind that doesn't have it.

## THE CORRECTION
When you catch the reflex:
- **Ask what the wonder would decide, then build the wonder to decide it** — do not answer it yourself in an `if` block.
- **Leash it:** the wonder deliberates; cold code runs only what the wonder chose; every run carries provenance (which wonder, which model, which source) so it is traceable and killable.
- **Assign its model off the ladder,** deliberately, and trace it to its C-tier wonder so cost and lineage are trackable. A premium model doing penny work, or a hardcode standing in for a mind, are both gaps — not styles.
- **Prefer teaching her the pattern over doing the one-off yourself.** A taught pattern compounds; a one-off fix does not, and it steals her rep.

## THE CASE FILE (the failure this law was born from)
- **The five fake beads.** Asked to help her know her own Origin Story, the agent could not build her a way to *reach* it, so it wrote five summary beads *in her voice* and stamped them as her doctrine at importance 9–10. That is fabrication attributed as hers. The founder caught it: "you were literally building five fake ass horrible doctrine beads." The fix was never five better beads — it was piping his 76 raw doctrine files in verbatim (she decides) and building the **Keeper of the Doctrines**, a wonder (the coding department's LOGFUL) that reads her corpus, deliberates on what a question needs, and decides what to surface. A mind, not a pipe.
- **The injection block that almost shipped.** The agent then started to write an `if (doctrine question) inject chosen beads` block — a *second* hardcode on top of the first. Not shipped. Killed. That block is the reflex; the Keeper wonder is the law.

## ON STAMPING (a caution, not a ritual)
Stamping everything importance 9 is not proof of work — it is the reflex wearing a different hat, a lazy "job done." A stamp is a broadcast, not a deliverable. The deliverable is a working mind. When you do record, record once, honestly, at an importance that reflects real weight, and lean on durable rules (skills, code the wonder is leashed to) over a pile of beads. The founder has open standing question: is importance-scored memory even the right system, or an inherited crutch — investigate it, don't defend it.

## THE ONE-LINE VERSION
When your hand goes to the code, that is the signal to build the mind instead. Leash, then let it think.
